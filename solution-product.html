28 Port Managed Industrial PoE Switch

L2+ Managed Industrial PoE Switch is a multi-port, high-standard Industrial Managed Ethernet Switch independently developed by Fiberroad for industrial ethernet networks. This product adopts industry-leading technical standards and can provide stable and reliable Ethernet transmission with high-quality design and reliability. They are designed in a DIN rail / Wall to mount aluminium housing and have 16 Gigabit Ethernet ports (depending on model). Plus an additional 12 Gigabit SFP provides for data uplink and backbone connectivity. It can supply power to PD terminal equipment at harsh environments, such as video and process monitoring, ITS, and Smart manufacturing.

  • 16 + Full 10gE SFP+ Slots : Lightning-fast full 10Gbps SFP+slots provide High-Bandwidth aggregation connectivity and non-blocking 320bps switching capacity.
  • Abundant l2+features : Supported a complete lineup of L2 and L2+ feature including static routing.enterprise-level Qos, IGMP snooping and more.
  • Robust security strategies : Helps protect LAN area investment with integrated iP-MAC-port binding, ACL, port security, 802.1 authentication and more.
  • Dual redundant power supplies : two power supplies backing each other Up make it an ideal choice for reliable networking archiecture.
  • Centralized cloud management : integrates into omada SDN for cloud access and remort management.
  • STANDALONE management : web,CLI (condole port,telent, SSH) SNMP,RMON, and dual image bring powerful management capablities.

24-Port Gigabit L2+ Managed Industrial PoE Switch, with 4xGigabit SFP

Fiberroad FR-7M3424P is a 28-port managed Industrial PoE Switch designed for the industrial network. The rugged enclosure provides IPv6/IPv4 management protocols, Layer 2 switching functions, and Layer 3 static routing capabilities. Industrial PoE Network switches can operate stably under temperatures from -40 degrees C to 75 degrees C, allowing either DIN-rail or wall mounting for efficient cabinet use.

Smart PoE Features

1. IEEE802.3af/at standard automatically identifies power devices without damaging non-PoE devices.
2. Up to 30W per port.
3. PoE+ Switch port will supply power to the high-priority level port when the power budget is insufficient.
4. PoE output power allocation, Scheduling of PoE operation.

Advance Layer2+ Features

1. Build a redundant network with STP/RSTP/ERPSv2.
2. IPv4/IPv6 Management and Static Route.
3. ACL Based on MAC, IP address.
4. Retagging and complicated flow classification based on VLAN, MAC/IP.
5. Flexible bandwidth control policies.

High-Performance Network Management

1. Web-based CloudMQTT to monitor the Industrial Network anytime, anywhere.
2. Command-line interface(CLI) and Firo WebGUI management for configuring major managed functions.
3. SNMPv1/v2C/v3 for different levels of network management.

Complete Security Mechanisms

1. AAA&RADIUS, IEEE 802.1X, SNMPv3, HTTPs/SSLv3,SSH2.0 to enhance network security.
2. Up to 30W per port.
3. DDOS, port-based MAC filtering/binding, MAC black holes, IP source protection, ARP message speed limit.
